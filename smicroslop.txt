Oh MicroSlop, my sweet MicroSlop,
Updating yourself in the middle of the night,
Rebooting my computer
And Start menu, all messy,
Filled with Candy Crush ads, yes,
I paid 25,000 taka for my desktop.
But, even so, you wish to sell me Cuisine™?
Windows 11, an unwanted gift indeed,
"You don't support your CPU," they did concede.
Five years old is not too good,
Sell your PC and get your profits from another hood.
Microsoft Edge, fixed on taskbar,
Pre-installed, pre-configured, oh, I see your scar!
I switch to Chrome, but you don't feel scared,
To ask me if I wish to change my ways, again and again, a hundred!
Cheers to you, MicroSlop, king of the game,
Bloatware and bug with a billion-dollar name,
Frustrating us to bits everyday,
But, since Windows 11, we just can't stray!
